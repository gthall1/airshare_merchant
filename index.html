WORD-OF-MOUTH v2.0

Airshare™ is a mobile app that makes it easy for consumers to gift (and be gifted) your business on a mobile phone! Now instead of just telling someone about a favorite local business, a user can share that business, creating an entirely new and personalized gift experience sure to make their recipient feel special.

HOW IT WORKS

Airshare’s video shopping experience exclusively features specialty retailers, mom-n-pop shops, and other small businesses that make a big difference in our local communities. No matter what type of business, no matter what kind of product or service you offer, Airshare makes it simple and secure to earn money and reach shoppers looking for local gifts and gift experiences, just like yours.

It’s FREE & Easy

1. CREATE YOUR LISTING

It’s free and easy to create a listing on Airshare. Describe your business in words, add photos or video, complete a few details, and your work is through. Once activated in the marketplace, now anyone, anytime, anywhere can purchase Airshares to your business!

  • GET STARTED
It’s FAST & Easy

2. ACCEPT AIRSHARES

Airshare delivers customers right to your store who have money to spend with you. At checkout, using the Airshare app, a customer will confirm the exact amount to redeem. Airshare’s secure redemption system means you never have to deal with cash directly. Using a phone, tablet or other internet-connected device, you or your staff approve the redemption, all in less than 10 seconds!

  • GET STARTED
Pay-for-performance only

3. GET PAID

You are paid automatically via ACH direct deposit after each validated redemption, minus the service fee. That means you pay but only when you receive funds from an Airshare redemption!
