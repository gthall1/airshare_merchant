When evaluating the Airshare fee structure and benefits to your business, consider these frequently asked questions:

  • +-  How much does Airshare cost?

    Airshare pricing is simple: you pay only for new sales. For every $1 in new sales via Airshare, you pay 8 cents, with a maximum fee of $5 per sale. So, a new $10 sale paid for with Airshare costs you 80 cents. A new $75 sale costs you $5, and so does a new $200 sale. And for all sales under $10, there is no cost to you at all! That’s right, they’re FREE!

  • +-  How do I redeem an Airshare?

    Redeeming an Airshare is easy. At checkout, a customer will confirm the exact amount to redeem, and initiate the redemption in the Airshare app. You or your staff then simply approve the redemption using a phone, tablet, PC or other internet-connected device, all in less than 10 seconds!

  • +-  How do I receive my money?

    All payments to Airshare merchants are processed securely through Airshare’s payment partner Dwolla, a world-class platform for secure marketplace transaction settlement. When you enroll with Airshare, you will automatically receive an invitation from Dwolla to enter your bank account information on their secure platform. Airshare pays you much like your credit card processor pays you. When a customer redeems an Airshare at your business, you authorize the redemption and the Airshare platform validates it, redeems it, and settles the amount with you via a bank transfer. We process all settlements the day of each Airshare redemption, and for the full amount of each redemption. It takes 1-2 business days for the funds to be deposited directly into your bank account. All redemption details are tracked and reported on your Airshare merchant dashboard. Airshare will then bill you once per month for the prior month’s total redemption fees. If you have any questions about our settlement process please contact us at business@getairshare.com

  • +-  What if we already have a gift card program?

    We encourage you to keep your existing gift card program. Typically, when your customers purchase your gift cards they do so with a credit or debit card, and you incur a transaction processing fee. Airshare’s redemption fee includes the processing & settlement fees. So, when comparing and contrasting gift card programs, be sure to subtract your actual credit card processing fee from Airshare’s 8% redemption fee. By way of example, if the standard processing fee that you pay today is 2%, then your net Airshare redemption fee is actually 6%, not 8%. Be sure to recognize that Airshare provides new, incremental sales at no upfront cost, no cost to issue, and the redemption fee is only paid on new sales. You can’t say any of that about your current gift card program now can you?

  • +-  How much more revenue can we expect?

    How much revenue you actually make will vary depending on your type of business, your product/service pricing, the season, demand, and other factors such as how well your staff promotes your eGift Card program to increase impulse purchases. But what we can tell you is that, on average, customers spend 24% beyond the value of their gift card. That means your average revenue for a $50 Airshare is actually $62.35. Backout the Airshare redemption fee, inclusive of transaction fees, and your net revenue on that same $50 Airshare redemption is $58.35.
